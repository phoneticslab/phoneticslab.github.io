*Bare*, *burn*, *Cark*? Earlier this week, Claire was invited to BBC Radio Lancashire and Radio Cumbria, where she talked about rhoticity and accent change in urban centres in Victorian Lancashire.

UK listeners can catch up on Claire's segment before the end of the month [on BBC Sounds (from 2:22:23)](https://www.bbc.co.uk/sounds/play/m002qfzm)!
