On 8th-10th April, the Phonetics Lab attended the [2026 Colloquium of the British Association of Academic Phoneticians](https://warwick.ac.uk/fac/soc/al/research/baap2026/), which took place at the University of Warwick. Memebers of the group were represented in seven presentations at the conference:

* **Sam Kirkham** & Patrycja Strycharczuk - AURORA model of formant-to-tongue inversion for didactic and clinical applications
* **Anton Malmi** & **Claire Nance** - Acquiring the acoustics and articulation of complex consonants in Estonian
* Simona Montanari, **Claire Nance** & Robert Mayr - Vowel production by Spanish-English bilingual children: An instrumental articulatory study
* **Claire Nance**, **Maya Dewhurst**, **Sam Kirkham** & Takayuki Nagamine - Articulation at the end stage of sound change: Derhoticisation across east Lancashire
* **Seren Parkman** & **Claire Nance** - The effect of cognition and motor control on voice quality across the lifespan
* Patrycja Strycharczuk, **Justin Lo** & **Sam Kirkham** - Articulatory strategy as a source of variation in acoustic vowel dynamics
* **Di Wang** & **Claire Nance** - Producing /l/ and /n/ from a merging L1 system: L1 Chongqing Mandarin, L2 Standard Mandarin, L3 English
